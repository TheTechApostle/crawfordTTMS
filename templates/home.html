Platform Features

Everything You Need to
Manage University Timetables

A powerful, role-based system built for Crawford University's academic scheduling needs.

Timetable Submission

Students submit course timetables with full details — venue, lecturer, time slots — for admin review and approval.

Admin Approval Workflow

Administrators review, approve, or reject submissions with notes. Students receive instant notifications of decisions.

Dynamic Categories

Admins create and manage timetable categories (Lecture, Lab, Seminar, etc.) that students choose from when submitting.

Department Management

Admin dynamically adds and manages university departments used in student registration and timetable filtering.

Admin-Controlled Accounts

Only administrators can create student accounts — ensuring controlled access. No public self-registration allowed.

Public Timetable Viewer

Anyone can view approved timetables by selecting department, level, session year, and category — no login needed.

How It Works

Simple. Structured.
Powerful.

Crawford TTMS is designed for smooth timetable management — from account creation to public schedule viewing.

Students Submit Timetables

Logged-in students submit timetables with course details, time slots, venue, lecturer, and select a category.

Admin reviews submissions, approves or rejects with notes. Students receive real-time notifications instantly.

5
Public Can View Approved Timetables

Anyone can browse approved timetables by department, level, session, and category — no account needed.
